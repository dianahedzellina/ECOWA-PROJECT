Inspiration Behind the Project

The inspiration for this project comes from the dramatic transformation and rapid growth of the e-commerce industry over the past two decades. E-commerce has fundamentally reshaped the way businesses operate and consumers interact with goods and services.

The Importance of Web Archiving in E-Commerce

Web archiving is a critical process for preserving the rapidly changing world of e-commerce. E-commerce platforms, websites, and applications are dynamic by nature; they are updated frequently to reflect new products, services, and technologies. Without proactive archiving, we risk losing a comprehensive understanding of how e-commerce has shaped our world.

Why We Choose to Preserve E-Commerce

Our decision to focus on preserving e-commerce stems from its unparalleled influence on the modern economy and its potential to shape the future.
